## For Paywalled Papers (Not Listed Above)

If you encounter paywalled papers, use one of these methods:

### Method 1: University Access
- Log in through your institution's library portal
- Use VPN if needed
- Search DOI on journal website

### Method 2: ResearchGate
- Go to https://www.researchgate.net
- Search paper title
- Click "Request full-text" → author responds in 24–48h

### Method 3: Email Author
Find author email in paper → send polite request (completely legal)

### Method 4: Unpaywall Extension
- Install: https://unpaywall.org
- Checks automatically for free legal versions
